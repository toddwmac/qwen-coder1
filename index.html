Self-Attention Mechanism

Self-attention allows the model to weigh the importance of different words in a sequence when processing each word.

Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax(QK^T / d_k)V

Where:
- Q (Query): What am I looking for?
- K (Key): What do I contain?
- V (Value): What information do I carry?

Positional Encoding

Since transformers don't have inherent sequence order like RNNs, positional encodings are added to input embeddings.

PE(pos, 2i) = sin(pos / 10000^(2i/d_model))
PE(pos, 2i+1) = cos(pos / 10000^(2i/d_model))

Training Stages

Modern LLMs undergo multiple training stages, each serving a specific purpose.

Pre-training

Pre-training is the foundational stage where models learn language patterns from massive datasets.

  • Objective: Next token prediction
  • Datasets: Web crawls, books, articles (trillions of tokens)
  • Compute: Thousands of GPUs/TPUs for weeks or months

Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT)

SFT teaches the model to follow instructions using high-quality human-written examples.

  • Objective: Learn to follow instructions
  • Datasets: 10K-100K curated instruction-response pairs

RLHF

RLHF aligns model outputs with human preferences using reward models.

  • Reward Model: Humans rank model outputs
  • PPO: Policy optimization to maximize reward

Alignment & Safety

Final stage focuses on ensuring safe behavior.

  • Constitutional AI: Self-critique based on principles
  • Red Teaming: Adversarial testing

Advanced Prompting Techniques

Effective prompting can significantly improve model performance.

Chain of Thought

Encourage step-by-step reasoning.

"Let's think step by step..."

Few-Shot Learning

Provide examples in the prompt.

Q: 2+2=? A: 4
Q: 3+3=? A: 6

Role Prompting

Assign a specific persona.

"You are an expert physicist..."

Tree of Thoughts

Explore multiple reasoning paths.

Self-Consistency

Generate multiple solutions and vote.

Retrieval Augmented

Combine with external knowledge.
